# O-List-Ecommerce-Performance-Dashboard
Analyzed 98k+ orders and R$16M revenue. Key insights include a 97% completion rate, 89% positive sentiment, and a 4.1 rating. Discovered that 77% of sales occur on weekdays, with credit cards as the top payment method. Built using Excel and Pivot Tables to optimize logistics and customer satisfaction.

Tools & Skills Used:
* Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Data Analysis & Visualization
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Dashboard Creation
* Business Insights & Reporting

Data Source: Olist E-Commerce Public Dataset

Key Insights & Analysis:
Customer Sentiment & Satisfaction :- The majority of customers are highly satisfied with their experience.
Order Fulfillment & Status :- The operations show high reliability with a vast majority of orders reaching completion.
Payment Preferences :- customers majorely prefer making payments through Credit Cards.
Order Trend :- Shopping activity is heavily concentrated during the week (77.1% on Weekdays vs. 22.9% on Weekends).
Top Performing States: Rio de Janeiro (RJ) leads in maximum payment values, followed by states like Espírito Santo (ES) and Mato Grosso do Sul (MS).

**Outcome:**
Delivered a comprehensive analytical report and dashboard that provided clear visibility into sales trends, customer behavior, and operational performance, enabling data-driven strategic decisions.
